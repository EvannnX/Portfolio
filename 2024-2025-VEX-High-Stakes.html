2024-2025 VEX High Stakes

NYU Shanghai Robotics Club participation in the 8th China University Intelligent Robot Creative Competition.

Context
NYU Shanghai · Robotics Club
Focus
VEX Robotics · Competition · Engineering
Stack
VEX V5, RobotC, Mechanical Design
Year
2024-2025

Case

Participating in the prestigious 8th China University Intelligent Robot Creative Competition (第八届中国高校智能机器人创意大赛) as part of NYU Shanghai Robotics Club.

Task

Design, build, and program VEX robots for the High Stakes competition challenge, focusing on autonomous and driver-controlled performance in complex game scenarios.

Competition Overview

The 8th China University Intelligent Robot Creative Competition (第八届中国高校智能机器人创意大赛) is one of China's most prestigious robotics competitions for university students. This year's VEX High Stakes challenge presents teams with complex autonomous and driver-controlled tasks that test both engineering design and programming capabilities.

NYU Shanghai Robotics Club is proud to participate in this national-level competition, representing our institution alongside top universities from across China. The competition provides an excellent platform for students to showcase their robotics skills and innovative thinking.

NYU Shanghai Robotics Club

As an active member of NYU Shanghai Robotics Club, our team brings together students from diverse academic backgrounds—engineering, computer science, mathematics, and design—to tackle complex robotics challenges.

The club emphasizes collaborative learning, hands-on experience, and innovation in robotics technology. Our participation in the VEX High Stakes competition demonstrates our commitment to excellence in robotics education and competition.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Autonomous robot programming and sensor integration
  • Mechanical design optimization for competition performance
  • Team collaboration and project management
  • Strategic game analysis and robot adaptation

Technical Implementation

Our VEX High Stakes robot utilizes the VEX V5 platform, featuring advanced sensors, motors, and control systems. The robot is designed to handle multiple game elements efficiently while maintaining reliability under competition pressure.

Technical Specifications:

  • VEX V5 Brain for autonomous and driver control
  • High-torque motors for precise movement and manipulation
  • Advanced sensor suite including encoders, gyroscope, and vision sensors
  • Modular design allowing for quick modifications between matches

Competition Strategy & Performance

The VEX High Stakes game requires robots to perform complex tasks including object manipulation, navigation, and strategic positioning. Our team has developed both autonomous and driver-controlled strategies to maximize scoring potential.

Through extensive testing and iteration, we've optimized our robot's performance for various game scenarios, ensuring consistent and reliable operation during competition matches.

Team Collaboration & Learning

This project represents the culmination of months of collaborative work within NYU Shanghai Robotics Club. Team members contributed expertise in mechanical design, programming, strategy development, and project management.

Key Learning Outcomes:

  • Advanced robotics programming and control systems
  • Mechanical design principles and optimization techniques
  • Competition strategy development and adaptation
  • Team collaboration and project management skills

Our participation in the 8th China University Intelligent Robot Creative Competition has been an invaluable learning experience, pushing our technical capabilities and fostering innovation in robotics education.
